Our Services

High-End Expertise

We bring your designer's vision to life with flawless execution for your residence.

Our team specializes in luxury finishes and complex builds. We collaborate closely with architects and designers to ensure every detail, from materials to finishing, is of impeccable quality.

  • Luxury finishes and custom materials
  • Complete end-to-end project management
  • Seamless coordination with designers
  • Respect for budgets and timelines

Commercial Excellence

Creating design-driven environments for restaurants, boutiques, and offices.

We understand the unique demands of commercial spaces. Whether it's a trendy restaurant or a sleek boutique, we deliver projects that combine aesthetics with functionality, all while adhering to strict building codes.

  • Restaurant and bar renovations
  • Boutique and office fit-outs
  • Turnkey project delivery
  • Adherence to commercial and safety standards

Exterior Renovation

Pressure-treated wood decks, cedar porches, and high-end exterior solutions.

Transform your outdoor areas into an extension of your home. Our expertise covers everything from custom deck design to facade renovations, using durable materials built to withstand the Quebec climate.

  • Custom deck design and construction
  • Patios and porches in wood or composite
  • Facade and siding renovations
  • Sustainable landscaping solutions

Disaster Recovery

Comprehensive post-disaster services to guide you through the reconstruction process.

When faced with water damage, fire, or any other disaster, our team reacts quickly to assess, secure, and rebuild. We work with your insurance company to turn adversity into an opportunity to improve your space.

  • Emergency response and securing
  • Damage assessment and estimation
  • Complete reconstruction and renovation
  • Coordination with insurance companies
